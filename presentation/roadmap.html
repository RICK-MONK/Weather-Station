Product Roadmap

A practical growth path for Smarty Weather Station, starting from strong monitoring and moving toward a fuller smart-environment platform.

PHASE 1
Monitoring First
  • stabilize sensing and display experience
  • refine dashboard views and control status
  • improve hardware presentation and enclosure
PHASE 2
Alerts And Actions
  • threshold-based alerts for heat, humidity, and dry soil
  • notification hooks for web or mobile
  • smarter warning and maintenance states
PHASE 3
Automation Layer
  • link soil readings to irrigation actions
  • add actuator control and queue execution
  • move from monitoring to response
PHASE 4
Scaled Product
  • cloud deployment and multi-device support
  • fleet dashboards for larger sites
  • analytics and prediction-ready data pipeline
